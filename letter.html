A letter for you, my love

Dear Cheran,

Happy Anniversary, my love. Can you believe it’s already been three years? Time really flew by. We met back in 2022, and even after everything we’ve been through, we still care about each other just as much, maybe even more now.

We’ve had our good days, our confusing days, and even some tough moments apart, but through it all, what we have has never changed. Our love just keeps getting stronger. So don’t stress too much, okay? You deserve to feel happy. And when things get hard, just come to me. You never have to deal with anything on your own. I want to be with you through everything, the good times and the rough ones too.

I just want you to know how loved you are. You’re so patient, kind, and you’ve always tried your best for me, even when I didn’t see it right away. You’ve made me feel loved in ways words can’t explain, and I’ll always be grateful for that.

I know we can’t celebrate together right now, but that’s okay. Our moment will come soon. Until then, just remember I’m always with you, no matter where we are. I love you sooo much.

Thank you for being you, for loving me, for staying, for never giving up on us.

Yours always, Oshadi
